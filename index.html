Echange Rate

Exchange Rate App for iOS:

  • supports Dollar, Euro, GBP, Yuan to Ruble exchange rates;
  • contains rate dynamics history graphs;
  • time intervals includes: week, month, quarter, year;
  • double tap to see rate delta for time interval.
